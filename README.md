# FUTURE_DS_01 – Retail Sales Performance Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

As part of the Future Interns Data Science Internship, I worked on analyzing a retail sales dataset to uncover valuable business insights and trends.

The goal of this project was to explore sales performance, customer behavior, profitability, and regional trends using Python-based data analysis and visualization techniques. By transforming raw sales data into meaningful insights, this project demonstrates how data can support better business decisions.

---

## 📊 Dataset Information

The project uses the **Superstore Sales Dataset**, which contains detailed information about customer orders, products, sales, profits, and geographic regions.

### Key Attributes

* Order Details
* Customer Information
* Product Categories
* Sales Revenue
* Profit
* Regions and States
* Order Dates

---

## 🎯 Objectives

The main objectives of this project were:

* Analyze overall business performance.
* Identify top-selling product categories and sub-categories.
* Evaluate regional and state-wise sales performance.
* Monitor monthly and yearly sales trends.
* Discover high-value customers.
* Examine profit generation across different categories.
* Create visualizations for better data interpretation.

## 🔍 Project Workflow

### 1. Data Collection

The dataset was loaded directly from a compressed ZIP file using Python.

### 2. Data Cleaning & Preparation

* Converted date columns into datetime format.
* Extracted year and month information.
* Prepared data for analysis and visualization.

### 3. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

Performed detailed analysis on:

* Total Sales
* Product Categories
* Sub-Categories
* Regions
* States
* Customers
* Profitability

### 4. Data Visualization

Created multiple visualizations to understand business performance, including:

* Sales by Category
* Sales by Sub-Category
* Sales by Region
* Top 10 States by Sales
* Monthly Sales Trend
* Yearly Sales Trend
* Top Customers Analysis
* Profit Analysis
* Correlation Heatmap
* Sales vs Profit Relationship
* Stacked Category Sales Chart

### 5. KPI Analysis

Calculated important business metrics such as:

* Total Sales
* Total Profit
* Total Orders
* Total Customers
* Average Sales Value
* Number of Product Categories

---

## 📈 Key Insights

Through this analysis, several valuable insights were identified:

* Certain product categories contribute significantly more revenue than others.
* Sales performance varies considerably across regions and states.
* A small group of customers generates a large portion of total sales.
* Seasonal trends can be observed in monthly sales patterns.
* Higher sales do not always guarantee higher profits.
* Profitability differs across product categories.
